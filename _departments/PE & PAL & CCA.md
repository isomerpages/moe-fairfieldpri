<h4><strong>Programme for Active Learning (PAL)</strong></h4>
<p>PAL, offered to Primary 1-2 students, provides a broad exposure of experiences to our students in the 4 domains: Sport &amp; Games, Outdoor Education, Performing Arts and Visual Arts. It offers a balance between the academic and non-academic areas in the school, and facilitates holistic and well-rounded development of the students.&nbsp;</p>
<p>One of the main features of PAL that sets it apart from our regular PE, Art and Music lessons is the emphasis on&nbsp;<strong>inculcating values and developing social and emotional competencies.</strong>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Values remain essential to shape the person&rsquo;s beliefs, attitudes and actions in whatever type of world the child finds himself in, in the future.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Through values education, students learn Responsibility, Respect, Resilience, Integrity, Care and Harmony. Together, these form the R<sup>3</sup>ICH Values.</p>

<h4><strong>School Teams</strong></h4>
<p>Physical Sports develop robustness, fair play and team spirit in students. The Visual and Performing Arts instil in students a sense of graciousness and an appreciation for the rich culture and heritage of a multi-racial society.&nbsp;</p>
<h4><strong>Uniformed Groups</strong></h4>
<p>Uniformed Groups aim to provide a holistic developmental programme for all P3-P6 students as they inculcate values to mould cadets into responsible individuals and valuable team members. The many opportunities to exercise leadership help our students to grow and mature; to show care and concern for those they lead, and to form bonds of friendship and trust.</p>
